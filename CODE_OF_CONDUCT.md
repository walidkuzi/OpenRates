# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors,
regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Standards

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and inclusive in all communication.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started.
- Accept and offer constructive criticism thoughtfully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Give credit for others' contributions.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Insults, derogatory comments, or trolling.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, contributions, or conversations that violate
these standards. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

Report incidents to the project maintainers. All reports will be investigated promptly.
